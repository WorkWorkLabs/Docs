# Personal Dashboard

### Product Overview:

Provides users with an integrated personal information management platform that aggregates personal information such as work, projects, payment history, and community participation, helping them track and manage their work and personal lives.

#### AI & Crypto:

* **AI Data Analysis**: AI analyzes users' work history, income, social activities, and more to help them optimize their career paths.
* **Cryptocurrency Payment History**: Displays all of a user's cryptocurrency transaction records for easy management and tracking.
* **Decentralized Identity Management**: Leverages blockchain technology to provide users with decentralized identity authentication and information storage.

#### Core Features:

* **One-Stop Personal Information Management**: Displays personal work, social, and financial status.
* **AI Career Optimization Recommendations**: Provides career development suggestions based on user behavior.
* **Cryptocurrency Payment History**: View all cryptocurrency transaction records.
