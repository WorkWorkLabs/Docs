# Productivity tool sales

### Product Overview:

WorkWork's productivity tool sales module is designed to provide efficient work tools and resources for digital nomads, remote workers, and self-employed individuals. These tools include, but are not limited to, project management software, task tracking systems, time management tools, and AI-assisted work platforms. Users can purchase memberships for these productivity tools to enhance individual or team productivity.

* **Token Credit Payment**: Users can use WorkWork's $WW token to purchase productivity tool memberships or additional AI credits, enabling decentralized payment methods and reducing cross-border fees and charges associated with traditional payment systems.
* **Crypto Payment**: Users can pay for membership fees and value-added services (such as AI services and additional storage) using cryptocurrency, ensuring global access to the platform.

### Core Features:

* **Crypto Payment**: Users can pay for products and memberships using $WW tokens or other cryptocurrencies.
* **Productivity Tool Subscription Service**: Different membership tiers are available, including Professional and Team versions, to meet the needs of diverse users.
