# Events

Product Overview:

Provides global online and offline events for digital nomads and remote workers, including webinars, skill-building courses, industry conferences, and more. Users can participate and share their experiences through the platform.

#### AI & Crypto:

* **AI Personalized Event Recommendations**: AI recommends relevant events based on user interests, industries, and historical behavior.
* **Crypto Payment**: Users can purchase event tickets, courses, and conference materials using cryptocurrency.

#### Core Features:

* **Event Creation and Management**: Users can create, manage, and promote their own events.
* **AI Recommendations**: Intelligently recommends events that users are interested in.
* **Crypto Payment Support**: Event fees can be paid for using cryptocurrency.
