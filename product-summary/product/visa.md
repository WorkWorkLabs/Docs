# Visa

Product Overview:

Provides global visa information and application services, helping digital nomads navigate diverse countries and regions.

#### AI & Crypto:

* **AI Visa Recommendation**: Based on the user's nationality, destination, and work requirements, AI recommends appropriate visa types and application procedures.
* **Crypto Visa Fee Payment**: Users can pay visa application fees in cryptocurrency.
* **Blockchain Evidence Storage**: Uses blockchain technology to ensure transparency in the visa application and approval process.

#### Core Features:

* **AI Smart Visa Recommendation**: Recommends the most suitable visa option based on user needs and conditions.
* **Crypto Visa Fee Payment**: Supports cryptocurrency payments for visa application fees.
* **Visa Process Transparency**: Blockchain technology ensures transparency and security in visa applications.
