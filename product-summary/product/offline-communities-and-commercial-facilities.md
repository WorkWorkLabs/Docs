# Offline Communities and Commercial Facilities

Product Overview:

WorkWork provides offline communities and nearby commercial facilities (such as coworking spaces) for digital nomads, helping them better integrate into local society. The platform connects digital nomad bases, coworking spaces, social events, and more around the world.

#### AI & Crypto:

* **AI Social Recommendations**: AI recommends suitable offline events and communities based on user interests, industries, and social behaviors, as well as local partners for networking.
* **Crypto Payments**: Payments for all offline community events accept both cryptocurrency and fiat, allowing users to easily pay for various activities.
* **Community Governance**: Leveraging blockchain and crypto technology to ensure transparency and engagement in community management.

#### Core Features:

* **Community Event Publishing**: Showcases and publishes global community events and collaboration opportunities.
* **AI Recommended Social Events**: Recommends offline social events based on users' work areas and interests.
* **Crypto Payment Solutions**: Pay for community fees or event tickets with cryptocurrency.
