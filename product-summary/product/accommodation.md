# Accommodation

#### Product Overview:

Provides nomad-friendly accommodation options worldwide, from shared spaces to apartments, allowing users to easily find a place that meets their needs.

#### AI & Crypto:

* **AI Smart Accommodation Recommendations**: AI recommends suitable accommodation options based on the user's work needs, budget, and preferences.
* **Crypto Payment Support**: Accommodation payments can be made via cryptocurrency, simplifying cross-border payment issues.
* **Smart Contract Transaction Security**: Ensures that accommodation booking and payment transactions are conducted through smart contracts, protecting the rights and interests of both parties.

#### Core Features:

* **AI Accommodation Recommendations**: Recommends the best accommodations based on user preferences.
* **Global Accommodation Payment Support**: Supports payment via cryptocurrency.
* **Accommodation Transaction Security**: Smart contracts ensure the security of accommodation transactions.
