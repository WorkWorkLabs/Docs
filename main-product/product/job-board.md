# Job Board

1. Job Board Product Overview:&#x20;

WorkWork's job board platform is designed to provide global job opportunities for remote workers, freelancers, and digital nomads. Through its AI-powered matching engine, the platform helps users find the best jobs for them and streamlines the recruitment process.&#x20;

AI & Crypto Integration:&#x20;

AI Job Recommendation Engine:&#x20;

Based on a user's skills, experience, interests, and goals, AI automatically recommends the most suitable jobs and provides personalized job search advice.&#x20;

Crypto Payment:&#x20;

Employers can pay salaries in cryptocurrency, providing remote workers with lower costs and cross-border payment convenience.&#x20;

Smart Contracts: Ensure the execution of employment agreements, automatically settle salaries, protect the rights of both parties, and resolve disputes on traditional platforms. Core Features: AI Job Matching: AI recommends relevant jobs based on a job seeker's profile and job preferences. Crypto Payment Support: Employers and job seekers can choose to pay or receive salaries in cryptocurrency. Blockchain Authentication: Important information such as job postings and resume updates is stored on the blockchain, ensuring data security and reliability.
